The Standard

We do one thing, completely.

American Crab Co. has spent over twenty years doing a single thing better than anyone else in the region: sourcing premium Louisiana blue crab and getting it to the DMV kitchens that depend on it — graded right, on time, at its peak.

We bring in prime Gulf blue crab straight from Louisiana, season after season, so our accounts get steady size and supply year-round — even when the Chesapeake is short or out of season. Same species, same sweet meat. It's how we hold a consistent grade when others can't, and why restaurants across the DMV have stayed with us for years rather than months.
