Our Story

Two decades in the blue crab business.

American Crab Co. is a family operation, and it has been from the start. For over twenty years we've done one thing, and done it completely: source premium Louisiana blue crab and get it to the DMV kitchens that depend on it.

We bring in prime Gulf blue crab straight from Louisiana, so your kitchen gets steady size and supply year-round — even when the Chesapeake is short or out of season. Same species, same sweet meat. We grade it by hand and move it fast, so what lands on your dock is exactly what your menu needs. That consistency is why restaurants stay with us for years.

"Anyone can sell you a crab. We've spent twenty years making sure it's the right one."

— Patrick Morrogh, Owner

The Standard

From the Gulf to your dock, in five steps.

Every crab we deliver passes through the same disciplined chain — the reason our quality never wavers, order after order.

  1. 01

    Harvest

    Pulled fresh from the Gulf by the Louisiana crabbers we've worked with for years.

  2. 02

    Inspection

    Every catch is checked by hand for weight, liveliness, and condition before it moves.

  3. 03

    Sorting

    Graded precisely to size and spec, so your order is consistent every single time.

  4. 04

    Distribution

    Packed cold and routed across the DMV on our own six-day delivery schedule.

  5. 05

    Delivery

    On your dock at its peak — graded right, on time, ready for service.
