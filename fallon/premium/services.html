Services

One shop for everything your vehicle needs — diagnosed honestly, fixed properly, and backed by a nationwide warranty.

Virginia State Inspection & Emissions

Fast, thorough safety and emissions inspections at an affordable price. If something needs attention, we show you exactly what and why — no surprises, no upselling.

  • Annual VA safety inspections
  • Emissions testing
  • Pre-purchase inspections before you buy a used car

Brake Service & Repair

Your brakes are the one system you never want to think twice about. We restore factory stopping power with quality parts — and stand behind them for 24 months or 24,000 miles.

  • Pads, rotors & calipers
  • Brake fluid flushes & line repair
  • ABS diagnostics & repair

Engine Service & Repair

From a rough idle to a rebuild, our technicians diagnose the actual cause before recommending work. That's how a repair stays fixed.

  • Check-engine light diagnosis
  • Timing belts, chains & water pumps
  • Engine repair & replacement

A/C & Heating

Virginia summers and January mornings both demand a climate system that just works. We service the full system, not just the symptoms.

  • A/C recharge & leak detection
  • Compressors, condensers & evaporators
  • Heater cores & blower motors

Steering, Suspension & Alignment

If the car pulls, wanders, or eats tires, we'll get it tracking straight — and your tires will thank you for it.

  • Computerized wheel alignment
  • Shocks, struts & springs
  • Tie rods, ball joints & bushings

Hybrid Service

Hybrids need a shop that understands both halves of the drivetrain. Ours does — without dealership pricing.

  • Hybrid maintenance & diagnostics
  • High-voltage system safety checks
  • Hybrid brake & cooling service
