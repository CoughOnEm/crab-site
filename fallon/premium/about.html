Twenty-five years. One standard.

Fallon Automotive started in 2001 with a simple promise: professional service at a fair price. Two locations later, the promise hasn't changed.

Our story

Built the slow way — on trust

Plenty of shops can fix a car. What's kept Sterling coming back to Fallon Automotive for 25 years is what happens before the wrench comes out: an honest diagnosis, a straight answer, and a price that's explained rather than defended.

We're driven by a commitment to excellent workmanship in every area of expertise — and by the belief that a repair shop should earn a customer for life, not just for one invoice. That's how a two-bay operation grew into two full facilities without a dollar of it coming from cut corners.

Milestones

How we got here

2001

The first bay door opens

Fallon Automotive opens in Sterling as a family-owned shop with a straightforward promise: do the work right, charge a fair price.

2017

Double the bays

Demand outgrows the original building. We move into a new facility with twice the service bays and far more parking.

2020

Joe's Auto Works joins the family

We take the reins at nearby Joe's Auto Works on Underwood Lane, adding four more bays and a second Sterling location.

Today

Two shops, one standard

Hundreds of five-star reviews later, both locations run on the same principle they started with. Some things shouldn't change.
