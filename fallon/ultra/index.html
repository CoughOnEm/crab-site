03 — The story

Built one
car at a time.

Fallon Automotive opened in 2001 with one promise: professional service at a fair price. Twenty-five years and two shops later, nothing about that has changed.

What started as a small family operation grew the only way that lasts — through work good enough that customers came back and brought their neighbors. Today both Sterling locations run under the same roof rules: diagnose honestly, fix it properly, stand behind it everywhere.

2001

The first bay door opens

Fallon Automotive begins in Sterling as a family-owned shop.

2017

Double the bays

Outgrew the original building — new facility, twice the capacity, more parking.

2020

Joe's Auto Works joins

The Underwood Lane shop joins the family, adding four more bays.

Now
